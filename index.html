Nolan Dey

I am a MASc student in Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I am advised by Bryan Tripp, Graham Taylor, and Alexander Wong. My thesis topic is interpreting tuning dimensions in deep neural networks. My research interests include using neuroscience to inspire neural algorithms, neural network explainability, and reinforcement learning. I also run a Deep Learning Reading Club

I completed my BASc in Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I completed machine learning internships at Mind Foundry, Apple, and Capital One and software engineering interships at Parabol, Connected, and Kik.
